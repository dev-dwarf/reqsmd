# Mission Operations Center

JPL shall maintain a **Mission Operations Center** for spacecraft commanding and monitoring.

## Functions

- Command generation and uplink
- Telemetry processing and monitoring
- Anomaly detection and response
- Science data distribution

Interfaces with:
- DSN for communications [[GND-01]]
- Navigation for tracking [[GND-02]]
- Science teams for data [[OPS-03]]
