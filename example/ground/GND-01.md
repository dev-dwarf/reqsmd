# Deep Space Network Support

The ground system shall provide **continuous communication coverage** via the Deep Space Network.

## DSN Stations

| Complex | Location | Antennas |
|---------|----------|----------|
| Goldstone | California, USA | 70m, 34m |
| Canberra | Australia | 70m, 34m |
| Madrid | Spain | 70m, 34m |

120° longitude spacing enables 24-hour coverage. Supports [[COMM-01]] and [[COMM-02]].
