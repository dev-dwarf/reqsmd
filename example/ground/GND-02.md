# Navigation and Tracking

The ground system shall determine spacecraft **position and velocity** to support trajectory corrections.

## Techniques

1. **Doppler tracking** - velocity along line of sight
2. **Ranging** - distance measurement
3. **Delta-DOR** - angular position (VLBI)

Navigation accuracy enables:
- Gravity assist targeting [[TRAJ-01]]
- Moon flyby precision [[TRAJ-03]]
- TCM planning [[TRAJ-02]]
