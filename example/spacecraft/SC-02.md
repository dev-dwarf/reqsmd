# Radiation Tolerance

All spacecraft components shall be designed to withstand the **intense radiation environment** of the Jovian magnetosphere.

## Design Requirements

- Total ionizing dose: >100 krad (Si)
- Single-event upset immunity for critical systems
- Radiation-hardened memory in flight computers

Critical for:
- Computer systems [[COMP-01]]
- Science instruments [[INST-01]]
- Trajectory near Io [[TRAJ-03]]
