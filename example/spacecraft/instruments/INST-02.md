# Infrared Spectrometer (IRIS)

The IRIS instrument shall measure **thermal emission and reflected sunlight** from planetary atmospheres and surfaces.

## Specifications

- Wavelength range: 2.5 to 50 μm
- Spectral resolution: 4.3 cm⁻¹
- Field of view: 0.25° circular

Supports atmospheric science [[SCI-01]] and surface temperature mapping for [[SCI-02]].
