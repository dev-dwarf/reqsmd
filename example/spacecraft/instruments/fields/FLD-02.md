# Plasma Science Investigation

The PLS instrument shall measure **plasma properties** of the solar wind and planetary magnetospheres.

## Capabilities

- Energy range: 10 eV to 6 keV
- Species: electrons and ions
- Angular coverage: multiple sensors

## Measurements

- Solar wind speed and density
- Magnetosheath plasma
- Io plasma torus
- Heliosheath properties ([[SCI-04]])

Works in conjunction with [[FLD-01]] for complete space environment characterization per [[SCI-03]].
