# Magnetometer Investigation

The MAG instrument shall measure **magnetic field vectors** throughout the mission.

## Configuration

- Dual triaxial fluxgate magnetometers
- Mounted on 13-meter boom (minimize spacecraft interference)
- Dynamic range: ±0.5 to ±20,000 nT

## Science Goals

1. Planetary magnetic field mapping ([[SCI-03]])
2. Magnetopause boundary detection
3. Interstellar magnetic field ([[SCI-04]])

One of few instruments operating throughout extended mission [[MISS-02]].
