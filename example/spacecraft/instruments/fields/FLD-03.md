# Plasma Wave Subsystem

The PWS shall detect **radio and plasma wave emissions** from planetary magnetospheres and the heliosphere.

## Frequency Coverage

- 10 Hz to 56.2 kHz (plasma waves)
- 1.2 kHz to 40.5 MHz (radio)

## Key Detections

- Jupiter's radio bursts
- Saturn kilometric radiation
- Heliosphere "hum" (interstellar medium)

Historic detection: *Voyager 1 recorded plasma oscillations confirming interstellar space entry in 2012* - validating [[MISS-02]] objectives.
