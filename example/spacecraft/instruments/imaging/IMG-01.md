# Imaging Science Subsystem

The ISS shall provide **high-resolution imaging** of planets, moons, and rings using two cameras.

## Camera Specifications

**Narrow Angle Camera:**
- Focal length: 1500 mm
- Resolution: 800×800 pixels (Vidicon)
- FOV: 0.424°

**Wide Angle Camera:**
- Focal length: 200 mm
- Resolution: 800×800 pixels
- FOV: 3.169°

Requires precise pointing from [[SC-03]] and data storage [[COMP-02]].
