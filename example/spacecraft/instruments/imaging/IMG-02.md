# Moon Surface Imaging

The imaging system shall obtain surface images of target moons at **resolution sufficient to identify geological features**.

## Resolution Requirements

| Target | Min Resolution | Key Features |
|--------|---------------|--------------|
| Io | 1 km/pixel | Volcanic activity |
| Europa | 2 km/pixel | Ice fractures |
| Titan | 5 km/pixel | Surface through haze |
| Enceladus | 2 km/pixel | Tiger stripes |

Trajectory must enable close approaches per [[TRAJ-03]]. Supports [[SCI-02]].
