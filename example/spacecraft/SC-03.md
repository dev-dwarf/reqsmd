# Attitude Control System

The spacecraft shall maintain **precise three-axis attitude control** for science pointing and communications.

## Capabilities

- Pointing accuracy: ±0.1 degrees
- Slew rate: up to 1 deg/sec
- Sun and star sensors for attitude determination
- Hydrazine thrusters for control

Shares propellant budget with navigation [[TRAJ-02]]. Essential for imaging [[IMG-01]] and communications [[COMM-01]].
