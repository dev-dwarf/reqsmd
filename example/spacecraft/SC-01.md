# Spacecraft Configuration

The Voyager spacecraft shall be a **three-axis stabilized platform** capable of supporting all mission science instruments and subsystems.

## Major Elements

- High-gain antenna (3.7m diameter)
- RTG power source on boom
- Science instrument scan platform
- Propulsion module

Supports primary mission [[MISS-01]] with power from [[PWR-01]] and communications via [[COMM-01]].
