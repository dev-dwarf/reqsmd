# Radioisotope Thermoelectric Generators

The spacecraft shall be powered by **three RTGs** using Plutonium-238, providing electrical power independent of solar distance.

## Specifications

- Initial power: ~470 watts at launch
- Power degradation: ~4 watts/year
- Design life: 5+ years (supports [[MISS-03]])

RTGs enable operations beyond Saturn where solar panels are impractical.
