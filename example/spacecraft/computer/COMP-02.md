# Data Storage and Playback

The spacecraft shall provide **digital tape recorder** storage for science data buffering.

## Capability

- Capacity: 536 megabits (8-track)
- Record rate: up to 115.2 kbps
- Playback rate: matched to downlink

Essential for:
- Storing data during planetary encounters
- Playback during DSN contacts
- Imaging sequences [[IMG-01]]
