# High-Gain Antenna Communications

The spacecraft shall maintain **continuous communication capability** with Earth via the Deep Space Network.

## Link Budget

- Antenna diameter: 3.7 meters
- X-band frequency: 8.4 GHz
- Transmitter power: 23 watts

At Saturn distance (~10 AU), supports data rates of **45 kbps**. At interstellar distances (>100 AU), rate drops to ~160 bps per [[COMM-02]].
