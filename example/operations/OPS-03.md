# Science Data Management

The mission shall **process, archive, and distribute** all science data to the research community.

## Data Flow

1. Downlink via DSN [[GND-01]]
2. Processing at JPL [[GND-03]]
3. Calibration by instrument teams
4. Archive in Planetary Data System

## Data Volume

- Primary mission: ~5 terabits total
- Extended mission: ongoing at ~160 bps

All Voyager data publicly available per NASA open data policy.
