# Golden Record

Each Voyager spacecraft shall carry a **Golden Record** containing sounds and images representing Earth and humanity.

## Contents

- 115 images of Earth and solar system
- Natural sounds (wind, thunder, animals)
- Music from various cultures
- Spoken greetings in 55 languages
- Encoded instructions for playback

*While not a technical requirement, the Golden Record represents humanity's message to any future finders* - supporting the inspirational aspects of [[MISS-01]] and [[MISS-02]].
