# Extended Mission Objective

Upon completion of the primary mission, Voyager shall continue to explore the **outer heliosphere** and interstellar space for as long as communication remains possible.

## Key Goals

- Characterize the heliopause boundary
- Measure interstellar medium properties
- Continue transmitting data via [[COMM-01]]

This requirement extends the mission lifetime well beyond the original 5-year plan.
