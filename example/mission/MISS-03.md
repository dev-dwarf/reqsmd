# Mission Duration

The spacecraft shall be designed for a **minimum operational lifetime of 5 years** to complete the primary mission, with design margins supporting extended operations.

Lifetime drivers:

1. Power system degradation ([[PWR-01]])
2. Propellant budget ([[TRAJ-02]])
3. Component radiation tolerance ([[SC-02]])
