# Primary Mission Objective

The Voyager spacecraft shall conduct **close-up studies** of Jupiter and Saturn, their moons, and their magnetospheres.

This is the foundational requirement that drives all other mission requirements including:

- Science objectives ([[SCI-01]], [[SCI-02]])
- Trajectory design ([[TRAJ-01]])
- Spacecraft capabilities ([[SC-01]])
