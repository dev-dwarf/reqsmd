# Moon Reconnaissance

The spacecraft shall obtain **detailed observations** of the major moons of Jupiter and Saturn.

## Target Bodies

| Planet | Priority Moons |
|--------|---------------|
| Jupiter | Io, Europa, Ganymede, Callisto |
| Saturn | Titan, Enceladus, Mimas |

Surface imaging requirements defined in [[IMG-02]]. Trajectory constraints in [[TRAJ-03]].
