# Heliosphere Boundary Science

The spacecraft shall measure properties of the **heliosphere-interstellar medium boundary**.

## Observables

1. Solar wind termination shock location
2. Heliosheath plasma properties
3. Interstellar magnetic field direction
4. Galactic cosmic ray intensity

This extended mission science supports [[MISS-02]]. Data return requires [[COMM-02]].
