# Magnetosphere Investigation

The spacecraft shall measure the **magnetic fields and charged particle environments** of Jupiter and Saturn.

Key measurements:

- Magnetic field strength and orientation
- Plasma density and temperature
- Radiation belt particle flux
- Magnetopause boundary location

Implemented by fields and particles instruments ([[FLD-01]], [[FLD-02]]).
