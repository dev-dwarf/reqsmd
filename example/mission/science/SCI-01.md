# Planetary Atmosphere Study

The spacecraft shall characterize the **atmospheric composition and dynamics** of Jupiter and Saturn.

## Measurements Required

- Temperature profiles
- Chemical composition (H₂, He, CH₄, NH₃)
- Wind speeds and cloud patterns
- Atmospheric pressure at multiple altitudes

Requires imaging system [[IMG-01]] and infrared spectrometer [[INST-02]].
